Dear TV (and service providers),

For too long, I've been oppressed by your evil ways:

  • Exhorbitant Fees
  • Unwieldy Contracts
  • All-Or-Nothing Subscription Models
  • Crushing Monopolies

I honestly can't take it any more. With the emergence of the iPhone, iPad, Apple TV and Android, you should keep up with the times, not try to kill innovation with scary legislation such as SOPA and PIPA.

So today. On February 14th. I pledge to cut the cord and break up with you TV. I have plenty of choices anyway – Denso, ShowYou, Plizy, Deja, NowBox, and many, many more.

Goodbye, and I hope this breakup will help you to change your ways.

Yours Sincerely,
