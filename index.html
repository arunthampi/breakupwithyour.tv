Break Up With Your TV

It's not you, It's me

Dear TV (and service providers),

For too long, we've been oppressed by ancient practices:

  • Exhorbitant Fees
  • Unwieldy Contracts
  • All-Or-Nothing Subscription Models
  • Crushing Monopolies

We honestly can't take it any more. With the emergence of the iPhone, iPad, Apple TV and Android, you should keep up with the times, not try to kill innovation with scary legislation such as SOPA and PIPA.

So today. On February 14th. I pledge to cut the cord and break up with you TV. I have plenty of choices anyway – Denso, ShowYou, Shelby, Plizy, Deja, NowBox, and many, many more.

Goodbye, and I hope this breakup will help you to change your ways.

Yours Sincerely,

A Frustrated TV User

P.S. On second thoughts, it isn't me, it is you.
